Web-cam bright-spot tracking

This page uses your web cam to track the brightest spot in the view. This is the first step in doing positional tracking.

Instructions:

  • Allow the page to have access to your web cam
  • Shine a bright light at your web cam
  • Move the light around
  • Use up/down or W/S or touch-drag up/down to change the "power" parameter. It controls image contrast.
  • Use left/right or A/D or touch-drag left/right to change the "threshold" parameter. It controls which pixels are counteds.
Close instructions and start!
